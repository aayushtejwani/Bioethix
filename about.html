About BioEthix

Specialty Pharmaceutical Company

BioEthix is a speciality pharmaceutical company engaged in development, manufacture and marketing of quality finished dosages in domestic and international markets. Established in 2000 with its Corporate Office in United States of America (USA) and Headquartered in Gurugram(Haryana)-India.

Who We Are

Bioethix is an organization spread over the globe and promoted by a group of Non Resident Indians who have interests in various business activities like stock market, Crude oil trading, Forex, real state, Health care and Pharmaceuticals.

In Pharmaceutical business we intend to expand our operation in Africa, Middle East and Latin America through various partnerships.

In India our business is owned and managed by a Non Resident Indian Mr. Manish Varshney. Mr. Varshney's curriculum sums up his experience in handling business activities in the fields of Pharmaceutical, Automobile and Oil & Gas industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to Serve Global Healthcare needs through Empathy, Innovation and Technology. We believe that to ensure sustained growth, we need to clearly understand our customer's needs and use cutting edge technology to present innovative solutions.

Corporate Social Responsibility

At BioEthix Pharmaceuticals, we believe that contributing back to the society is not only a RESPONSIBILITY but a COMMITMENT. Our little value addition to the betterment of society is a part of our mission, in line with our commitment to human health.

Through the years, BioEthix has strived to make the world around it a better place. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is not just an integral part of our business but devotion; the promise of a brighter future for every life we touch.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, patient focus, and quality guide every decision. We consider it our duty to contribute a share of our earnings towards health benefits to the less privileged, rural welfare to make life easier for rural poor and several charitable activities to do our part towards the welfare of society as a whole.
